80% of student pilots drop out.

Think about it: four out of five student pilots don't even surpass the very first milestone to their prospective professional career. That's a staggeringly high rate of failure, but why? There are two primary causes of student pilots giving up: the massive cost of flight school, and a lack of structure in their training.

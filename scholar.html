Online scholarship options

There are all kinds of scholarships available online for people of all demographics and backgrounds. Here is a list of scholarship providers for flight training:
Aircraft Owners and Pilots Association (AOPA) CareerOneStop Civil Air Patrol (CAP) Epic Flight Academy Experimental Aircraft Association (EAA) Latino Pilots Association (LPA) LeRoy W. Homer Foundation National Air Transportation Association (NATA) National Gay Pilots Association (NGPA) The Ninety-Nines (The 99's) Regional Airline Association Women in Aviation International (WAI)

Local scholarship options

Scholarships aren't only offered online. Local scholarships are often available through local chapters and can be far less competitive than online scholarships.
